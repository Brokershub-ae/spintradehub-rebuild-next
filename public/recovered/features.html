Buyers can submit RFQs to multiple suppliers, compare quotes, and negotiate prices and lead times within the platform.

Secure payment options, invoicing and order confirmations tailored for B2B workflows.

Match buyers with brokers and traders, track leads, commissions and manage brokerage agreements.

Localized UX and customer support tailored for businesses operating in Greece.

Order history, spend analytics and supplier performance reports to help procurement decisions.
