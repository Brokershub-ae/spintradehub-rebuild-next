Frequently Asked Questions — B2B Industrial Marketplace

What is SpinTradeHub?

SpinTradeHub is a B2B marketplace for buyers, traders and brokers of industrial products in Greece — specializing in lubricants, bearings, V-belts and related components.

How do I request a quote (RFQ)?

Use the RFQ feature to submit your requirements (product, quantity, delivery location). Your RFQ is sent to relevant suppliers who respond with quotes you can compare.

Are suppliers verified?

Yes — suppliers on the platform have company profiles, and we collect certifications and ratings to help buyers select trusted partners.

What payment and invoicing options are available?

The platform supports standard B2B invoicing workflows. For production-ready payment features we integrate with payment providers or ERP systems as required.

Do you handle logistics and delivery?

We provide logistics integrations and estimated lead times. Final delivery is arranged between buyer and supplier; we assist with local carrier options within Greece.

How do I get help or a sales contact?

Use the Contact page to reach sales or support. For large procurements we offer direct sales assistance and broker introductions.
